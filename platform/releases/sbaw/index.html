Single Business Ads Widget

One of the main benefits of using the OwnLocal Platform is that a small business can get a nice place on the web to display their latest print ads in a stunning way. This is specially handy for those business that don't have a website, but those that do have one often requested the ability to display these same ads in their own website.

That's why we're thrilled to introduce a new type of widget that will allow businesses to embed their latest print ads anywhere on the web – including their own website.

Our new Single Business Ads Widget displays a specific business' ads in two different types of views - either as single featured ad or as a stream/feed.

To find a SBAW, simply go to any business page on the OwnLocal Platform that has active ads. Once you're there, scroll down towards the bottom of the page and click on the Embed button located on the right:

This button opens a small window with a widget preview and two different text areas:

The Widget URL is handy for embedding in website builders that make iframe embedding simple, such as Sidengo.

Most users will likely use the Embed Code – a small snippet of code that can be pasted anywhere HTML is accepted.
