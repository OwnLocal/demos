Widget Update Release Text

This release merges pull request #266 into master. It introduces a redesigned and rebuilt widget to please our partners that want horizontal (panoramic and wide) widgets, along with our signature sidebar sizes. The new widget has a refreshed design, displaying more information in the same space.

On wide resolutions, it displays both businesses and ads off the bat, making better use of the extra real estate.

On even wider resolutions, it horizontally aligns the content to avoid awkward sizes.

It can still get short and narrow...

...and now it also works when it's short and wide. The screenshot below shows the popular 728x90 ad size.

Partners can also now choose to display ads by default, instead of businesses.

You can play around with the new widget by going to our preview page.
