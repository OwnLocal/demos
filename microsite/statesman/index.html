AboutX

The Antique Gallery of Round Rock is Austin’s premier Antique Mall. With 30,000 square feet of antiques, there is enough shopping to here to fill your day. We feature American, English, French, Primitive, Religious, Mid-Century Modern and Western antiques and collectibles of all kinds. But the selection doesn't stop there, because our dealers offer a tremendous amount of glass ware and vintage décor to help you decorate any house.

Conveniently located off I-35 in Round Rock we are short drive from anywhere in the Austin area. We offer ample parking and there are great restaurants close by to dine at while you are in town.

Open 7 days a week. Our expert staff can assist you in finding the perfect antique and collectible for your collection or just to fit your lifestyle.

Appraisal Services

Whether you are settling or dividing an estate, establishing market, insurance or replacement value or you want to be informed of the value of pieces you own, we can provide you with an accurate appraisal report to include a detailed inventory, values and photos. Or maybe what you need is less detailed, such as a Verbal Approximation Assessment.

Jewelry Repairs
