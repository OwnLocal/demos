AboutX

EF Tire and Auto Repair has been Broward County’s choice for tires and complete auto repair, service and maintenance since 1979. Our staff of ASE certified master technicians are qualified and trained on all vehicle makes and models, and most of our staff members have been with us for over 20 years. That’s over 300 years of combined experience … not just working on cars, but working on cars together, right here in Pompano Beach.

From a simple burned out light bulb to a complete engine overhaul or the most complex computerized systems diagnostics, no job is too big or too small and every job is done right, the first time, every time. Unlike probably any other auto repair shop anywhere, every repair and service performed at EF Tire and Auto Repair is double checked for accuracy by a shop foreman upon completion. And, before any vehicle is returned to a customer, it’s double checked for cleanliness. No one works harder to earn your business.

EF Tire and Auto Repair also offers all brands of tires at the lowest prices available. We will meet or beat any competitor’s advertised tire price.

We honor most extended warranties, provide free estimates, nationwide warranties and free shuttle service. Why go to the dealer, lose your car for days and pay more when EF will save you time and money?
