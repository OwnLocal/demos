About Us

The ECM Post Review is a paid circulation newspaper published each Wednesday that serves the communities of North Branch, Stacy, Harris, Sunrise, Almelund, Rush City, and Rock Creek.

ECM Publishers, Inc. is a publishing, printing, digital media and distribution company that operates in Minnesota and western Wisconsin. Started in 1976 by founder Elmer L. Andersen, the company has grown from one paid circulation weekly newspaper and shopper to its current operations which include 49 publications. The market includes more than 650,000 homes.

You can learn more about us in our corporate website.
